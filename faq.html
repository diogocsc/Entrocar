How can I pay for a given service?

The coin in Use in this system is the Minute. After a service is provided, the person that collected the time shall access Entrocar platform, look for the request, and Report the Exchange. This Exchange shall be confirmed afterwards by the owner of the Request. You should agree with him the ammount of time just after delivering the service.

What info should I insert into the Request?

Insert as much info as possible related to the work to be done : The address or area you must/should/can meet, the date and time, the number of expected hours, special tools needed, tasks that may have some difficulties (weight lifting, dealing with sensible objects, etc.)

What happens if something bad happens to me during the exchange ?

A lot of things may happen, and you shall be self-conscious and responsible for everything related with the exchange. Entrocar does not take account for the exchange problems that may arise. Entrocar suggests people try to check the references of the other party if they are in some kind suspicious something wrong may happen. Entrocar is provided as Is, and is based on Trust and on individual empowerment
